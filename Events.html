High School Hockey Challenge at Hamilton Lake

The annual High School Hockey Challenge took over the scenic Hamilton Lake on November 15, bringing together Year 9 and 10 students for a fun and competitive end-of-year sports day. With the lake’s beautiful surroundings providing the perfect backdrop, the event was a fantastic opportunity for students to showcase their skills, enjoy the outdoors, and celebrate the school year’s end.

Highlights

  • Teams from multiple schools competed in fast-paced, friendly hockey matches.

  • Family and friends cheered from the sidelines, soaking up the festive atmosphere.

  • Special activities included skills challenges, sportsmanship awards, and a prize ceremony.

  • The event encouraged teamwork, fitness, and school spirit, all while enjoying the natural beauty of Hamilton Lake

    • Testimonial

    "It was such a great way to finish the school year! Getting outside, playing with friends, and enjoying some friendly competition right by the lake. The atmosphere was electric!" – Emma T., Year 10 participant

    Hakarimata Summit Challenge – In Collaboration with FitLife Gym

    The Hakarimata Summit Challenge teamed up with the local favourite FitLife Gym for an exciting weekend of outdoor fitness and community spirit. Participants took on the stunning Hakarimata trails with a choice of four tiers, designed to suit all fitness levels, from casual walkers to seasoned hikers. Each tier offered unique rewards and incentives, motivating everyone to push their limits while enjoying one of the region’s most iconic natural spots.

    Challenge Tiers:

  • Tier 1: 1.8 km walk to the trail start and back

  • Tier 2: 3.8 km scenic loop trail

  • Tier 3: 4 km climb up and down the famous 1,400 steps

  • Tier 4: Full 8 km summit climb and loop

    • Highlights

  • Strong community turnout with participants sharing encouragement across all tiers

  • Stunning views from the summit that left everyone feeling accomplished

  • Post-challenge meet-up at the gym with trainers and nutrition experts offering tips and prizes

    • Testimonial:

    "The 1,400 steps were tough but so worth it! The gym perks are a fantastic bonus to keep the momentum going indoors after conquering the trail." – Jason M., Tier 3 finisher

    The Dog Fair at Innes Commons

    The Dog Fair at Innes Commons was a joyous Saturday afternoon gathering for dog lovers and their furry friends. This social event created a vibrant space where dogs could mingle off-leash and owners could connect, share stories, and discover local dog-friendly products. With delicious food stalls and several small New Zealand dog brands showcasing their unique merchandise, it was a perfect blend of community spirit and canine fun

    Highlights

  • Wide range of food vendors offering treats for humans and pups alike

  • Stalls featuring local NZ dog brands with toys, treats, and accessories

  • Friendly dog meet-and-greets and fun activities like agility demos and photo booths

  • Tickets available through a popular third-party platform at $15 each, making it easy for families to join in

    • Testimonials

    It was amazing seeing so many happy dogs and meeting other owners in such a relaxed, fun environment. Plus, I found some great local products I can’t wait to try with my pup!" – Laura P., event attendee
